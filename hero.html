This is a website about my hero


My hero is Andy Murray.
Click here for a webiste about him.

Andy Murray is a Scottish tennis player. He was born in Glasgow, Scotland in 1987. He is a professional tennis player who has won three Grand Slam titles which include two at Wimbledon and one at the US Open. He plays mostly singles tennis, but is also known for playing doubles tennis. In addition to Andy Murray, his brother Jamie Murray is also a skilled and professional tennis player, but is more known for doubles tennis.
I chose Andy Murray as my hero because of his dedication to the tennis world. When I was younger I would spend Sunday afternoons watching him play at Wimbledon or the open at the time. I think that it is important for young people to see others who have such strong dedication to a sport or passion. Also, in recent years he has not been able to play as much as he wants to due to injury, yet is still trying to continue to play. I think that the love he shows for tennis inspires me to find something that I can feel that passionately about.
