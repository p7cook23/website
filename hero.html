Andy Murray is a Scottish tennis player. He was born in Glasgow, Scotland in 1987. He is a professional tennis player who has won three Grand Slam titles which include two at Wimbeldon and one at the US Open. He plays mostly singles tennis, but is also known for playing doubles tennis. In addition to Andy Murray, his brother Jamie Murray is also a skilled and professioanl tennis player.
